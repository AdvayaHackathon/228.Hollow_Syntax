Spellbook Instructions

1. Select alchemical equipment from the panel to add to your cauldron

2. Drag equipment to position it in your workspace

3. Click on magical ingredients to add them to your selected vessel

4. Turn on the Dragonfire burner by clicking on it

5. Experiment with different magical combinations

Try These Enchantments:

  • Neutralization Spell: Mix Dragon's Bile and Phoenix Ash with Fairy Dust
  • Dissolution Charm: Add Mermaid Tears to Moonwater
  • Heat Incantation: Use Dragonfire with various solutions
  • Precipitation Magic: Mix Blue Dragon Scale with Phoenix Ash
  • Transmutation: Observe Goblin Iron in different solutions
